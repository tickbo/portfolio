Introduction

Zhao Jiasen is a Digital Media Arts student at Xiamen University, graduating in 2026. The work engages with moving image, sound, and photography, often focusing on perception, time, and bodily experience. With an interest in experimentation and observation, the practice values process over fixed outcomes. After graduation, the intention is to work within the creative industry, continuing to explore how media can shape experience and attention..

I focus on duration, repetition, and the act of sustained looking or listening, allowing similar questions to be explored from multiple sensory perspectives.

My work often bridges the gap between digital precision (Max/MSP) and the vulnerability of the physical body, creating immersive environments that invite the viewer to slow down and observe.
